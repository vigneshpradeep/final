About Us

Our Story

Established in 1987 and headquartered in Pathirappally, Alappuzha, we are leading exporters of Coir yarn and Coir products. Specializing in Grass Mats for prayer, yoga, and beach use, we manufacture Coir Pith, Coir Fibre, Geo Textiles, Coir Logs, and more using cutting-edge technology for flawless products.

Our Mission

To produce durable, cost-effective quality products through innovative technologies, infrastructure modernization, and continuous improvement in working conditions.

Our Vision

To maintain transparency and deliver internationally acceptable quality products while upholding the highest standards in all our operations.
